# CSS

element1+element2
> 抓取element1下一個**element2**元素

element1~element2
> 抓取element1之後**element2**元素

[attribute]

[attribute|=value]

[attribute^=value]
[attribute$=value]
[attribute*=value]
:empty
抓取標籤無任何東西


::first-letter
::first-line
:first-of-type
:in-range

:invalid
抓取input驗證type屬性不對

:nth-last-of-type(n)
